Steve Mariott

Vocals · Guitar

The frontman for British hitmakers the Small Faces singer/guitarist Steve Marriott was born January 30, 1947 in London.There he met bassist Ronnie Lane, agreeing to jam with his band the Pioneers.

Ronnie Lane

Bass Guitar

Ronnie Lane (along with guitarist Steve Marriott) co-founded the British mod group the Small Faces in the mid-1960s, helping to guide them to the top of British charts with his clever songwriting.

Kenney Jones

Drums

Born September 16, 1948 in the East End of London, England, Jones took up the drums at the age 13. Kenney Jones has been a member of two of England's most beloved bands the Small Faces and Who.

Ian McLagan

Keyboards

A keyboard player conversant in R&B, blues, and roots rock who earned an estimable reputation as both a sideman and a bandleader, Ian McLagan was born in Hounslow, Middlesex on May 12, 1945.
